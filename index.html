About Me

Hi there! I'm Fargol. I work as an IT developer in Canada Revenue agency and I study Computer science. Outside my day job, I have a passion for aerial arts. Based in Ottawa, I began my aerial journey training at Aerial Antics and Ottawa Circus School. While my specialty is aerial hoop (lyra) and I tried trapeze, I’m deeply interested in the broader world of aerial arts and movement. My training focuses on building a strong foundation in technique while exploring fluidity and expression. I love combining grace and power. I’m eager to grow not only as a performer but also as a future instructor, and I hope to share the empowerment that aerial arts has given me.
