About Me

Hi! I'm Fargol, an aerial hoop student based in Ottawa. I began training under Anastasia Parkhomenko at Aerial Antics and continued at Ottawa Circus School. I'm currently at an intermediate level, working on moves like the gazelle, double knee hang, and mermaid spin. I'm passionate about combining strength and elegance through movement and I'm always eager to grow as a performer and instructor.
